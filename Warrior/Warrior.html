Warrior Class

Characteristics

  • Weapon Type: Greatsword, Axe, Hammer (swing speed, aa combo)
  • Secondary: Gauntlet
  • High mobility
  • Has quick and easy abilities that combo nicely
  • Fairly durable in most scenarios
  • Has a variety of specializations/abilites
  • Low mana costs (except LK)

(Aaron)         The base class for your standard RPG Warrior. The Warrior class itself includes mostly cheap/quick physical attacks that can very easily be put into combos. Crazy mobile with their sprint, as being able to run quickly from location to location makes a difference when it comes to getting rank SS since dungeons are time-based. They specialize into the sword slashing Swordsman, or heavy swinging Mercenary.

Swordsman deviate even further, going into sword combo-heavy gladiators who have higher speed and single-target physical dps, and the slower, harder hitting Lunar Knights that mainly throw AoE magic sword slashes. The Swordsman has a large variety of dashes and momentary stuns caused by chain combos.

Mercenaries on the other hand become the axe-wielding Barbarians who do high physical dps through spin attacks and armor breaking abilities, or the cc explosive Destroyer that smashes shockwaves at enemies with a hammer. Destroyers will typically do less dps but take less damage and can taunt. Mercenaries also have howls that buff themselves and allies defensively and offensively. All in all they contain a load of disruption and AoE physical damage.

(Kody)-High HP, high phys att

        If an MMORPG doesn’t have a warrior, it’s not an MMORPG. The warrior in Dragon Nest is a super-mobile DPS character that focuses mainly on whittling the enemies’ health down in close range. They can wield the Greatsword, Hammer, or Axe, and specialize into tankier disruptors or into quick damage dealers. This class is one of the fastest and mobile characters in the game, if not the most, because of their dash skill accompanied by a low cooldown tumble. They only have one party buff, Lifesaver, which lets team members survive on 1 HP for a short amount of time.

Notable Skills:

Impact Wave- a ranged shockwave that knocks up opponents and can hit enemies on the ground. Since it's magic damage based, it's mainly used by Lunar Knights a combo starter or a quick attack that can be used immediately and up close. At lvl 6, the shockwave increases greatly in size. At a certain range, this ability has a sweet spot where the most shockwaves will hit and do the most damage.

Rising Slash-a dash attack into an upward lift. Gladiators will sometimes use this move as part of their combo and it can lead to other quick upward slashes or the air combo.

Destructive Swing-a leap into the air into a downward aoe slam. A skill used by any of the physical damage Warrior classes; it's good at finishing small mobs at the end of a combo, and can also hit knocked down enemies.

Brush Off- a nice quick cleanse that removes 3 debuffs. Just pop it whenever you see that you have one (red buff).

Lifesaver- for one minute, anyone who'd normally die will instead stay alive for another 15 seconds. Use it when someone's in trouble but keep in mind the 5 minute cooldown.

Tumble, Dash, and Aerial Evasion- utility moves; roll to dodge attacks or start sprinting, sprint to get around the map, surprise attack to knock back enemies when getting back up, aerial evasion to recover in the air (decent cooldown).
