Bulletin/Tips:

Do note that for any guide, we mainly describe the base classes and their builds assuming you've reached your 2nd specialization or are about to. If you aren't at that level yet, don't worry about the skill descriptions yet and use the skill reset scrolls and have fun with your build, but pay attention to how many you have left. More info in the "Useful Info" page.

Final Damage is the greatest source of dps; near equivalent to % damage increase. This is why Ruler weapons are the strongest at lvl 80 for damage.

Quickest way to make money at higher levels is to have 15000 Achievement points and buy stamps daily to sell valuable bound items, or mats.
