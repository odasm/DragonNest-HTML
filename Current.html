Current Patch Rundown

This patch includes a couple balance changes along with the addition of the Dark Avenger class.

Sharpshooter: (PVE) Increased dmg on 4 skills, CDR on 2, and added Crit Resist debuff on 1.

Sniper: You can aim where to put Deathbloom but not when it explodes. (PVE) 4 EX skills are now Wind Arrows triggered by Crit.

Pyromancer: Hot Rod+ hitlimit removed, allows Tumble during cast.

Ice Witch: (PVE) Ice Witch (+Super Armor Defense), (PVE) Chilling Mist+ (+Damage increase)

Saint: (PVE) All relics are tougher/stronger and have additional effects. Holy Shield(+range) Divine Dash(additional ability)

Shooting Star: (PVE) Splash (-DMG; a LOT), (PVE) Chemical Grenade (Crit Resist debuff reduced)

Dancer: Dance of Ecstasy 3 (self only buff) (-HP/regen)(+Phys/Elemental DMG). (PVE) General Dawnblade (+DMG)

Spirit Dancer: (PVE) Praetor (+DMG)

Taoist: Heavy PVP nerfs, I suggest taking a look.

Lencea: Counter Spin (-Super Armor Defense)

Lancer: (PVP) Twirling Chop (-Invincibility time)

Dragoon: (PVP) Backflip Pierce, Forceful Bounce, Charging Sweep (-Super Armor Defense)
