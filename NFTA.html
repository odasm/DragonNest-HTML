FAQ

Q: What does it mean when PVE/PVP aren't specified?

A: PVE is PVE only, PVP is PVP only, and no specification is both.

Q: When are you gonna update the page?

A: That's a good question.. I'm not totally sure about that myself yet but depending on how busy I am but since Dragon Nest doesn't update/change meta all that fast, it should be whenever we feel something has changed whether its the game or our opinions. The "Updated for ___" should tell you if something's changed recently. The 3R Revamp is a huge change so it would take some time if I decided to do it.

Q: How come some class pages have their icons at the top and some don't?

A: If there's an icon, that means that either have us have played that class and think we probably know enough to speak about it from personal experience. No icon means we're writing about it from what we've observed or read about, but have never actually played.
