Sorceress Class

Characteristics

  • Weapon Type: Staff
  • Secondary: Grimoire, Orb, Puppet (different stats/auto attack types)
  • Strong aoe magic damage
  • Deals fire, ice, dark, or neutral magic damage
  • Average to above-average mobility through blinks
  • Features a large variety of status ailments and cc
  • Extremely squishy, and sometimes needs to be at the close or midrange to be most effective

(Aaron)         The Sorceress is a standard RPG mage that focuses on blasting opponents with magic at a mid-range. They have generally solid aoe damage in the form of lazers, gravity orbs, fire blasts, or ice shards. Different specializations will have different abilites, such as freeze capabilities, burn DoTs, high cooldown reduction, or suction cc. They aren't too crazy in the buff department but their cc generally makes up for it. They specialize into the fire/ice Elementalist or the dark/neutral Mystic.

Elementalists, obviously, have the fire skill tree and the ice skill tree. The fire tree contains various forms of fireballs and fire waves that set enemies aflame. The ice side has a variety of freezes and slows that can lock-down their enemies. Both are squishy and neither has that much mobility but they make up for it in either in a ton of AoE damage or cc.

Mystics split between dark magic damage gravity balls (which allows them to make use of Void Blast) or neutral magic damage lazers. Their teleport ability goes decently far, making their mobility noticeably better than Elementalists. They also have nice cc through their AoE time stop ability, and a magic mirror that protects them and debuffs enemies.

(Kody)-Highest magic defense, high magic dmg with main stat intellect

         Another staple of mmost MMOs haha see what I did there? As an arcane witch that can specialize in 3 elements along with a neutral magic class, the sorceress’ main role is to DPS and support with crowd control effects.

Notable Skills:

Glacial_Spike- an ice bolt that does decent damage and freezes enemies. A great filler skill since freezing enemies can multiply your damage to those targets; you can get extra hits off while they're frozen if you deal enough they'll even take shatter damage, though I don't currently know how powerful that is. Every mage maxes this ability above all others for it's utility except for the Chaos Mage, which makes use of Void Blast.

Void Blast- forms a gravity ball that explodes at a set distance. It's a cheap filler AoE ability taken specifically for Chaos Mages since they used dark magic; everyone else will take Glacial Spike over this. You'll find the other skills (Fire Burst, Wheeling Staff, and Shock Wave) to be quite underwhelming compared to Glacial Spike or Void Blast. Anyone play League? This is basically Syndra's Q, except it doesn't stick around and you can't control the range.

Blink, Power Blink, Rising Blast, and Aerial Evasion- utility moves; blink to dodge attacks, "rising blast" to knock back opponents when getting up, aerial evasion to recover in the air (decent cooldown). Power Blink lets you blink despite being paralyzed by something.
