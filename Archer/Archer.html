Archer Class

Characteristics

  • Weapon Type: Shortbow, Longbow, Crossbow (range, fire rate, damage)
  • Secondary: Quiver
  • Long range arrows or short range kick combos
  • Strong late game damage (Wind and Warden)
  • Super squishy but evasive
  • Higher crit chance abilities
  • Solid mobility
  • Mix of single target/aoe damage

(Aaron)         The Archer base class seems somewhat underwhelming until it's lategame, but it at least introduces you to what it's all about; fancy arrow shots and swift kick combos.

Sharpshooters are the classic archer type including various arrow shots depending on if you're a Sniper (physical, standard sniping/rain of arrows) or Warden (explosive/magic arrows going in different directions), so if you like the idea of bombarding your enemies with your various types of arrows from long distance, pick a Sharpshooter. They've got some pretty nice damage and move/action speed buffs as well. You also get the option of picking between longbows and crossbows (different range/damage).

The Acrobat is a close combat archer that only really uses it's bow for pulling enemies closer or for shooting enemies in the air; otherwise it features long range dashes, flip kicks, and aerial melee attacks that can be chained together quite nicely. They also have a damage buff, but instead of speed they have cool down reduction. The Archer class itself features some arrow shots and kicks but only a few of which will actually be used later on.

(Kody) -Low defense, high crit

        The classic ranged DPS of any party, the archer specializes in shooting barrages of arrows or a flurry of close range kicks and flips. (I hate the archer because you can’t touch these fkers).

Notable Skills:

Twin Shot-a quick one two double shot typically used by Snipers as cheap dps. It's a essentially a filler move thats longer range than your autoattack, can hit things on the ground, and occassionally stuns the target.

Magic Arrow-a straight shot with a small explosion on impact. It has the same uses as Twin Shot, except it's magic based and therefore maxed by Wardens instead.

Willow Kick-a short-ranged spinning kick that leads into a knock up. Typically taken by Acrobats to help lower the cooldown of their ultimate, and can be used inside combos. Some prefer to take multishot (shoot 7 arrows in a cone straight ahead) instead but it's more of a preference on how you want to combo your skills.

Wisdom/Fury of the Owl-free crit chance buffs! The first one, Wisdom of the Owl, boosts your party's crit chance by a huge amount for a short period of time (lasts 24s, 120s cd) while Fury of the Owl boosts it slightly, but permanently (lasts 180s, 10s cd).

Somersault/Moon Kick- one knocks enemies up for combos and the other knocks airborn targets back down. Mainly used by Acrobats for those reasons.

Tumble, Dash, and Aerial Evasion- utility moves; roll to dodge attacks, pin wheel to knock back opponents when getting up, aerial evasion to recover in the air (decent cooldown).
